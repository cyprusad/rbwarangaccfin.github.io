Network Connectivity

Due to involvement of cloud computing technology, this software just needs network connectivity. One can operate it from any place of the world and at any time. 24 x 7. The data can be accepted from various devices. It can be said it’s like an e-mail id which can be operated if one has internet connection. QuickBooks can be accessed from various devices including PC, Tablet, Mobile, etc. provided are connected to the internet.

To Access QuickBooks Online, Login to QuickBooks from any Internet connected Mac, PC or Phone. Work from the Office, Home, or on the Road. Mobile access is also available limiting to the customers. Using Computer browsers, users can have complete access to perform from anywhere, at any time, any of the tasks including the following:

  • Add and edit customers, vendors and employees.
  • Create and email invoices.
  • View Balance sheet, and Profit and Loss Reports.
  • View bank account and credit card balances.
  • Access vendors, customers and employees list.

Online Banking

For Online Banking, Link your QuickBooks account to your bank and automatically download all your account transactions from a growing number of Indian banks. This way you can reduce manual data entry errors and virtually eliminate reconciliation errors. Quick Books is completely secure.

Instantly Access Reports and Company Snapshots. QuickBooks reports give you valuable insight into your company's finances so you can make better business decisions, from basic financial reports to reports for specialized business activities. See the state of your business instantly with the Company Snapshot, a single screen that shows exactly how your business is doing.

Quick Books Automatic data backup system and centralized servers secures your data from sudden loss. Security system on par with banking services ensures that it is safe with us.
