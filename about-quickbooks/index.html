Uses of QuickBooks

QuickBooks is a specially designed software made for Small and Medium size businesses and Start – up companies. QuickBooks is useful generally for Trading Sector and Service Sector Businesses.

Major Uses of QuickBooks are:

  • Extremely adaptable to most small businesses. All documents and reports can be customized to the business needs. Reports can be readily exported to other applications.
  • QuickBooks is easy to use once properly setup, the software does an excellent job of recording, organizing and reporting the financial information of the business enterprise in accordance with established accounting standards and methods.
  • It is very stable and reliable. When you compare all the features and functionality of this software in relation to its cost, there is simply no other product in the market at this level which offers a better value for the small business enterprise.
  • QuickBooks allows users to create invoices to track how much money is owed to them. This information can easily be pulled up to find out which customers owe money and exactly how much they owe. Old invoices can also be pulled up to determine when an invoice was paid in full. This computerized process avoids piles of cumbersome paperwork.
  • QuickBooks allows users to keep track of how much money the company is spending on supplies, utilities, lunch meetings and other company expenses through the Expenses widget.
  • QuickBooks can easily save contact information for repeat customers and vendors.
